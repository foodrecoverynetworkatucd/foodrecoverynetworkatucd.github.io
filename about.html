ABOUT US

 

The Food Recovery Network at UC Davis is a student-run club part of a national organization comprised of 230 other colleges across 44 states. Collectively, more than 2.7 million pounds of food have been recovered and 2.2 million meals have been donated. Our own home chapter was established in 2014 and we are proud to be a part of the largest student movement against food waste in America. As a club, we strive to enable members to create tangible impacts in their community, on and beyond campus, build valuable professional skills, and make invaluable connections.
