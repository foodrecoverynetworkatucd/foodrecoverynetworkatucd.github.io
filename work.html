WHAT WE DO

Weekly Recoveries

We recover from the dining commons and campus food retail markets twice a week and donate the food to local homeless shelters Davis Community Meals and Fourth & Hope.

Football Recoveries

We deliver hot food from the concession stands to Davis Community Meals after football games.

Solano Park Free Farm Fare

We partner with YOLO Food Bank to help distribute excess produce from the farmer's market every Saturday to community members in need.

Volunteer and Socials

We have volunteer events at soup kitchens, community gardens, and community harvests. We also have fun outside of work-- with movie nights, BBQ's, and other seasonal activities!
